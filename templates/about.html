Built for clarity in a noisy world

OneTab is not just another file manager. It’s an attempt to remove friction from how we store, organize, and access our digital life.

Why this exists

Most tools today try to do everything — and end up doing too much. The result? Cluttered interfaces, confusing workflows, and wasted time.

OneTab was built with a different mindset: keep it simple, make it fast, and remove anything that doesn’t add real value.

“Simplicity is not a feature. It’s the product.”

Built by a student

OneTab is being built by a student who is learning, experimenting, and pushing to create something meaningful — not just another project.

This is not the final version. It’s a starting point — with a long road ahead.

🎯

Clarity over complexity

Every feature should make things simpler, not harder.

⚡

Speed matters
